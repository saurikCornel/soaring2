How to Play

The rules are simple but require precision and strategy:

  • Guide airplanes to the designated runway (marked as "Runway")
  • Direct helicopters to the helipad (marked as "Helipad")
  • Avoid collisions between aircraft by carefully planning their paths
  • Use drag gestures to create custom flight paths for each aircraft
  • Complete levels within the time limit to earn bonus points

Features

  • Manage up to 10+ aircraft simultaneously in advanced levels
  • Dynamic weather effects like fog, rain, and windstorms
  • Customize gameplay with adjustable difficulty and visual settings
  • Unlock special aircraft skins and unique airports
  • Compete for high scores in endless mode
  • Enjoy stunning aerial visuals and immersive sound effects

"Soaring Eagle" combines strategic planning with quick reflexes. As you progress through increasingly complex levels, you'll need to master the art of air traffic control while ensuring every aircraft lands safely.
